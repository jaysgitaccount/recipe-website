Oi Muchim (오이무침)

Description

An easy and delicious side dish to accompany a meal with rice. I stumbled upon this on social media and it turned out to be really good! When I find myself with too many cucumbers, I get the urge to make this. You can make large amounts and store it in the fridge, somewhat like kimchi. Great for depression!
The original recipe is in video format, so I'm storing it in text form for convenience.

Original recipe found here, by Jasmine Pak.

Ingredients

  • 8 cucumbers
  • Salt

For the sauce:

  • ½ cup green onions
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1.5 tbsp white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp korean chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tsp white sugar

Method

  1. Thinly slice or smash cucumbers into bite-size pieces.
  2. Pat dry, toss with salt, and let sit for 15 minutes.
  3. Rinse off salt and pat dry again.
  4. Transfer to another bowl or container.
  5. Dump in all of the sauce ingredients.
  6. Shake well.
  7. Serve immediately, or store in fridge overnight for extra flavor.
